Matches Spring 2017

Interdisciplinary Design Studio, Computer Science Department, UIUC


connecting people to other people through common interests


tools: user interviews, paper prototyping, storyboarding, Sketch

the challenge: how can a mobile app help with play?

user interviews: what does "play" mean to different people?

brainstorm: who are we designing for?


Our solution: Matches, a Tinder-inspired social platform where users can discover activities nearby and organize events with new people who share the same interests. Activities can range from videogames to sports to dancing--anything that involves socialization.

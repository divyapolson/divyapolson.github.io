reflection


My contributions were front-end development and lead UX design. Aside from implementing the front-end in React, I also got the opportunity to interact with data in our MongoDB database. If we had more time, we would have developed an in-application chat so users could discuss specifics of their trade or negotiate further (instead of linking to Facebook Messenger). We also would implement a rating system so that users can review other users’ services and rate their transaction. With a few more iterations, it would then be useful to do some further usability testing and identify areas of improvement. Overall, I liked the originality of our service-based marketplace and believe it could be popular among college-age students.
